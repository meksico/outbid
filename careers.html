Advertiser Account Manager

Description

MaxBounty is seeking driven individuals to build and maintain long-lasting relationships with our advertising clients. Your primary role with the company will be working with advertisers to produce campaigns that our affiliates will promote. You will learn about the performance marketing industry and become the main contact for both small and large businesses. You will set up and manage their marketing campaigns, maintain strict compliance, oversee budgets, and grow each account to its fullest potential. You will work well as part of a team, while self-managing your time to reach out to your clients daily. It is essential that you have no problem communicating over the phone, or through email and Skype messaging.

A post-secondary education and experience in account management, sales, digital marketing, or affiliate marketing is considered an asset.

Currently this position is fully remote as our office is closed due to COVID-19.

Duties
  1. Maintain continuous contact with advertisers to grow client relationships and achieve their desired sales goals.
  2. Set-up and manage performance marketing campaigns.
  3. Communicate with Affiliate Managers regarding campaign details and restrictions in relation to affiliate promotion.
  4. Attend industry tradeshows to meet with potential and existing clients to communicate company benefits and strengthen current relationships.
Required Skills and Competencies
  1. Multi-tasking.
  2. Problem solving.
  3. Computer and Internet skills.
  4. CRM (Customer Relationship Management) experience.
  5. Excellent written and oral communication in English.
  6. Ability to take Initiative.
Benefits
  1. Full time.
  2. Base salary plus commission.
  3. Full health and dental benefits paid 100% by employer.
  4. Work from home flex days.
  5. Catered lunch on Fridays.
  6. Team building events.
  7. Employee lounge with XBox.
Location

Ottawa, ON
Canada

Covid-19 Considerations

At this time our office is completely closed and all travel suspended. All interviews, training and work will take place from your home until it is deemed safe and prudent to return to the office.
